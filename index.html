Brief Explanation

Stockheimer is a "defend the castle" multiplayer game.
Defend the castle against waves of enemies to win the game.
*** This is currently built as a "tech demo". Most features need to be controlled manually, such as spawning enemies, castles, PvP, ect. These can be controlled on the bottom of the screen when you login.
Controls
WASD + Mouse
